Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 17, 2026

Ascend ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal-growth iOS application published by Berk Demirok. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

  • User-generated content: Categories you select, actions you complete, XP, streaks, and preferences. All stored locally on your device via AsyncStorage.
  • Purchase data: Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat. We receive an anonymous user ID and entitlement status — never your payment details.
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA): Collected only if you grant permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt. Used by Google AdMob to serve relevant ads.
  • Device info: Push-notification token (Expo Notifications) to deliver daily reminders you opt into.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or location.
  • We do not create user accounts or require sign-in.
  • We do not upload your action history to any server.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: Displays ads to free-tier users. AdMob may collect device identifiers per its own privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat: Manages in-app subscriptions. See RevenueCat's privacy policy.
  • Expo / EAS: Used for push notifications. See Expo's privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

All personal progress data is stored on-device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes it. We retain no server-side copy.

5. Children's Privacy

Ascend is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Your Rights

Because data is stored locally, you have full control. You can delete all data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in iOS Settings.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. The "effective date" at the top will reflect the latest revision.
